# Privacy

## What information does Prompto collect?

Prompto and its developer take your privacy very seriously.
Beyond the information installer methods provides to developers that covered
provided by the package manager privacy policies,
we use _no third-party analytics or advertising frameworks inside the executable_.
Prompto logs no information on you and has no interest in doing so.

## What information does the Prompto website collect?

The Prompto website uses Application Insights [custom events][ai] to log anonymous usage data.
We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Prompto does not collect, transmit, distribute or sell your data.

[ai]: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-monitor/app/api-custom-events-metrics#trackevent
